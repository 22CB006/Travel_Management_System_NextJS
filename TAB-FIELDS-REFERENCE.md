## Visual Indicators

### Active Tab
- Background: `#cedde7` (light blue-gray)
- Text: `#29383e` (dark blue-gray)
- Icon: Dark colored

### Inactive Tab
- Background: `rgba(1,1,1,0.56)` (semi-transparent black)
- Text: White
- Icon: White

### Form Container
- Background: `#cedde7`
- Padding: `10px`
- Border Radius: `10px`

### Form Fields
- Background: White
- Padding: `12px 14px` (standard) or `20px 23px` (large)
- Border Radius: `4px`
- Label: `#a3a3a3` (neutral-400)
- Value: `#1e1e1e` (dark gray)

### Search Button
- Background: `#29383e`
- Text: White
- Size: `143px × 68px`
- Border Radius: `8px`
